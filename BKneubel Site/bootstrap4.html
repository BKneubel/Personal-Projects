Urban Sustainability

        These are my sustainability assessments of Cleveland, Ohio and Rocky River, Ohio that I wrote for my Urban Sustainability class at University of Illinois. I will discuss some unsustainable features of both locations, as well as solutions to these issues. I have learned a lot about the history of Cleveland and Rocky River and am now aware of many aspects of the cities that are done well and aspects that leave room for improvement. Overall, I believe that writing these assignements truly helped my knowledge of sustainability as well as gain more familiarity with my hometown.
